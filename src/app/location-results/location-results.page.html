No Food No Money is a non profit initiative to fight with hunger issues.

It helps by tracking GPS location of person who needs food and shows these locations to nearby people. Near by people, NGO's, Authorities can the help in need for food.

We don't save any personal data of any user, we only save location (latitude & longitude) of users who choose to get help for food. For any queries or suggestions, please mail us at nofoodnomoney@gmail.com.

This initiative depends on your location and we couldn't access your location. Please enable your location to use this site.

Why we need your location ?

If you are selecting "I need food", we will show your location to people around you upto 5 kms and if you are selecting "I can help", we will show locations of people who need food around you upto 5 kms.
