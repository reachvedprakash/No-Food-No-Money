No Food No Money is a non profit initiative to help people who can't buy food.

How this works ?

It takes location & phone number of person in need and shows it to all persons who can help in 5 km radius. People who want to help can call them or reach their location to provide food.

If you need food

This site will need your phone number and location. Both will be shared with the people around you so they can help you. You need to allow access to your location if not allowed yet.

If you can help

This site will just need your location to show you all persons in need for food in a 5km radius around you. It doesn't save location or show it to others. You need to allow access to your location if not allowed yet.
